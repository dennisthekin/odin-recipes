Oreo Milkshake

Description

This milkshake is easy to make, super kid-friendly and delicious - a favorite on a hot day!

Ingredients

  • 6 chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo)
  • 3 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 3/4 cup milk

Steps

  1. Blend chocolate sandwich cookies, ice cream, and milk together in a blender until smooth. Pour into 2 glasses.
